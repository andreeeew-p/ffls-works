# sidekick

> **Macro & Office Assistant Utility**
> 
> Sidekick is an ultra-lightweight Windows productivity utility designed to automate repetitive office tasks and organize your work environment with ease.

---

## ■ Workspace-Friendly Dual Driving Modes

Two distinct interface modes are provided to seamlessly blend into your workspace without blocking your screen real estate.

1.  **Side-Pin Mode**
    A compact, vertical layout with Always-on-Top support. Optimized for side-by-side multitasking alongside Excel, web browsers, or internal ERP systems.
2.  **Background Tray Mode**
    Closing or minimizing the window sends Sidekick straight to the system tray, completely out of sight. In this mode, all global hotkeys and macro listeners remain fully operational.

---

## ■ Key Features

*   **Macro Recorder & Keyboard Automation**
    Easily record manual repetitive processes including mouse clicks (screen coordinates), keystrokes, and custom delay offsets. Saved macro sequences trigger instantly via global hotkeys (`Alt+1` ~ `Alt+9`). Quick slots (Alt+1~4) let you assign hotkeys instantly on the fly.
*   **Smart Clipboard Manager**
    Keeps track of your text and image clipboard copy history. Features a line-break trimmer (cleans up formatting and unwanted newlines) and an Excel-friendly TSV conversion table copy feature.
*   **Screenshot & Offline OCR (Text Extraction)**
    Capture any screen area with a global hotkey (`Alt+S`) and extract text from the image 100% locally on your device, without requiring an active internet connection. Operates securely offline without data leakage concerns.
*   **Cross Image Search**
    Perform reverse image searches of captured frames via global search portals including Google, Bing, and Yandex.
*   **Integrated Toolbox & Quick Launcher**
    Gather Windows essentials like Calculator and Notepad, alongside a Spell Checker and Unit Converter, all in one place. Add your favorite local program paths or web addresses to use Sidekick as a quick launcher.
*   **100% Local Processing & Transparent Design**
    Clipboard logs and captured images are never uploaded to external servers, processed entirely on your local PC for maximum security. Features a translucent glassmorphism design that integrates beautifully with Windows dark/light themes.
